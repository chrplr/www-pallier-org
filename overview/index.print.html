Project Overview

What is this project?

This project combines the speed of Hugo for the marketing/landing pages and the power of Sphinx for technical RST-based documentation.

Why two engines?

  1. Hugo: Fast, great for blogs and landing pages.
  2. Sphinx: Best-in-class for LaTeX PDF generation and technical cross-references.
